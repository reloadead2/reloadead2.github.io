Chapter 1: The Beginning

The morning sun rose over the hills, casting a warm glow across the sleepy village. Birds sang softly as the world stirred awake.

Inside a small cottage, a young boy laced his boots, excitement shining in his eyes. Today marked the start of his long-awaited journey.

He looked back once more at his home before stepping out the door, unaware of the destiny that awaited him beyond the horizon.
